Gold

We Buy & Sell Gold Jewelry in NYC

10k–24k chains, rings, bracelets, and scrap.

Gold offers reflect karat, weight, and market — we test and explain melt vs. resale value when pieces are designer or collectible.

What Gold Jewelry do you buy?

We buy authentic Gold Jewelry in good to excellent condition — walk in to our East Village shop for a same-day evaluation and cash offer if you accept.

How much is my Gold worth?

Value depends on authenticity, condition, rarity, size or specs where relevant, and current NYC resale demand. We explain the number before you decide.

Do I need a receipt to sell

No. Receipts and original packaging can help but are not required. We authenticate and price based on the physical item.

Do you buy used or pre-owned

Yes — gently used pieces are welcome when condition supports resale. Heavy damage may reduce the offer or be a pass.

How do you authenticate

Our buyers inspect brand-specific details — materials, labels, construction, serials or date codes, and packaging when present — in front of you at the counter.

How long does selling take?

Most items take a short in-store review. You can leave the same day with cash if you accept the offer.

Is there any obligation to sell?

Never. Evaluations are free with zero pressure. Decline the offer and you owe nothing.

Do you also sell authenticated

Yes. We buy and sell authenticated jewelry in NYC — every piece we sell is inspected before it hits the floor.
