FAQ

Common Questions

Quick answers about selling collectibles at East Village Buyers.

Do I need an appointment?
Walk-ins welcome. For large collections, text photos to 917-608-8939 first.
How do I get paid?
Same-day cash on the spot when you accept our offer.
Can I text photos for a quote?
Yes — text clear photos to 917-608-8939 for a rough estimate. Final offers confirmed in person.
Do you buy opened or displayed figures?
Yes — displayed pieces are accepted if clean and undamaged. Sealed in box gets the best offer.
Does the original box matter?
Yes — OG box significantly increases value for KAWS, Bearbrick, Funko, and Lego. We buy without box at reduced price.
Do you buy bulk or full collections?
Yes — bring your entire collection. We evaluate each piece individually and make one offer for the lot.
Do you buy common Funko Pops?
Common Pops have limited resale value. We focus on vaulted, chase, convention exclusives, and grails.
Do you buy loose Pokemon cards?
We focus on sealed product and graded slabs. Raw cards evaluated case by case for high-value singles.
Do you buy opened Lego sets?
Sealed gets the best price. Complete opened sets with instructions and box are evaluated case by case.
Do you buy fakes or replicas?
No. We only buy authentic collectibles. We verify before making any offer.
Do you buy vintage Hot Wheels?
Yes — Redlines, early castings, and vintage carded cars. Paint and wheel condition drive value.
Can I buy and sell in one visit?
Yes — sell for cash and browse our authenticated inventory in the same trip.
