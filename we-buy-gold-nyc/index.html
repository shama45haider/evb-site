How We Check the Value of Your Gold

Nothing happens behind a curtain. Here's the exact process we run on every piece, right at the counter, while you watch.

Done in under 5 minutes

Step 1

Look It Over

We check for a hallmark stamp like 10K, 14K, or 18K — a starting point we always verify further.

Step 2

Run an Acid Test

A tiny scratch and a drop of karat-matched acid tells us fast if the gold is what it claims to be.

Step 3

Confirm Electronically

For pieces we don't want to mark, an electronic verifier reads purity with zero damage.

Step 4

Weigh & Check Live Price

We weigh the piece in grams and multiply against today's live spot price for gold.

FAQ

Gold Selling Questions, Answered Straight

The questions we hear most, including the ones people are usually too polite to ask out loud.

Is East Village Buyers a pawn shop?
No. A pawn shop lends you money against your item and holds it until you pay the loan back. We buy your gold outright. You get cash, we get the gold, and that's the end of the transaction — nothing to pay off later.
How much is my gold worth right now?
It comes down to karat and weight, multiplied by today's spot price. Check the live price widget above for a general idea, then bring your piece in for an exact number.
What is 14K gold worth per gram?
14K is 58.3% pure gold, so it's worth about 58.3% of the day's spot price per gram. That percentage stays fixed, but the dollar number moves with the market every day.
Do you test my gold in front of me?
Every time, no exceptions. We run an acid test and an electronic reading right at the counter so you can watch the whole process before we talk numbers.
Do you buy broken gold jewelry?
Yes. A broken clasp, a bent ring, a single earring — none of that changes how much gold is actually in the piece, so it doesn't change what we pay.
Do you buy dental gold?
Yes. Crowns, bridges, and fillings are often high karat gold and test just like anything else. Bring them in.
Do I need a receipt to sell gold?
No receipt required. We only care what the piece is made of, not where it came from. You will need a valid government ID, which is standard for this kind of sale in New York.
Do you buy gold-plated jewelry?
Gold-plated pieces have very little actual gold in them, so we usually pass. If you're not sure whether something is plated, solid, or gold-filled, bring it in and we'll test it for free.
Is there a limit on how much gold you'll buy?
No. We've bought everything from one broken chain to a full estate collection. For a large amount, text photos to 917-608-8939 ahead of time so we can plan for it.
How fast do I get paid?
Same day. As soon as you accept our offer, you're paid before you leave the counter.
Do I need an appointment?
Walk-ins are always welcome. If you're bringing in a large or high-value collection, a heads-up text just helps us prepare, though it's not required.
Are you licensed?
Yes — NYC DCA license #2070477 (Vintage USA Inc, DBA East Village Buyers).
Can I text photos before I come in?
Yes, text clear photos to 917-608-8939 for a rough idea. We'll still need to weigh and test the piece in person for a final number.
