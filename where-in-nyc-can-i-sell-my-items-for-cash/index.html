Whether you're cleaning out a closet, settling an estate, or just need fast cash, New York City has no shortage of places that claim they'll "buy anything." The truth is more complicated — where you should sell depends heavily on what you're selling, and the payout, speed, and hassle vary wildly between options.

Pawn
Pawn Shops
Fast cash · Low offers · Varies widely

NYC pawn shops will take almost anything quickly, but their offers skew low — they need margin to resell at a markup, and many prefer to loan against your item rather than buy it outright. If speed matters more than getting full value, a pawn shop works. If you want a fair price, a direct buyer will almost always beat a pawn shop's offer.

  • Fast cash in hand
  • Offers tend to be the lowest in the market
  • Often structured as a loan, not an outright sale
Consignment
Consignment Shops
No upfront cash · Split of eventual sale

Consignment stores display your item and pay you a percentage once it sells — usually 40-60% of the sale price, and only after it actually sells, which can take weeks or months. There's no guarantee it sells at all, and you don't get cash today. This only makes sense if you're not in a hurry and want to try for a higher price.

  • Can net a higher price for the right item over time
  • No cash until (and unless) the item sells
  • Store takes a significant percentage cut
  • No guarantee of a sale at all
Mail-In
Mail-In Buyback Services
Convenient · Locked-in quotes · Slow payout

Mail-in services (common for electronics, watches, and some jewelry) give you an online quote, then you ship the item and get paid after inspection. If the condition doesn't match exactly what you described, the offer can drop before you see any money. Payout typically takes a week or more after they receive your item.

  • No need to leave home to get an initial quote
  • Quotes can drop if condition doesn't match exactly
  • 1-2 week turnaround from shipping to payment
  • You're without your item for the whole process

How Much Will I Get for My Items?

It depends on the category, the specific item, its condition, and current market demand. A few things that affect nearly every offer, regardless of category:

  • Condition — wear, damage, and functionality all factor directly into your offer
  • Authenticity — items we can quickly verify as genuine get faster, stronger offers
  • Completeness — original boxes, papers, or accessories can add value for watches, sneakers, and designer goods
  • Market demand — trends shift, and some items are simply worth more in NYC right now than others

The only way to know exactly what your items are worth is to bring them in, or text us photos first for a real, specific number — not a vague range.
