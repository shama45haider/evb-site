FAQ

Common Questions

Quick answers about selling jewelry at East Village Buyers.

Do I need an appointment?
Walk-ins welcome. For large estate collections, text photos to 917-608-8939 ahead.
How do I get paid?
Same-day cash or check for larger transactions. Payment immediately after you accept.
Can I text photos for a quote?
Yes — text clear photos to 917-608-8939 for a rough estimate.
Do you buy gold-plated jewelry?
Gold-plated has minimal gold content — we typically pass. Gold-filled pays less than solid gold. We test and explain.
Do you buy dental gold?
Yes — dental gold is typically high karat. We weigh and test it the same as any other piece.
Do you buy lab-grown diamonds?
Lab-grown have very limited resale. We focus on natural diamonds but can evaluate any stone.
Can I sell my engagement ring?
Yes — engagement rings, wedding bands, and diamond jewelry. Free evaluation, no pressure.
Melt value vs. resale value?
Melt is based on metal weight and purity. Resale applies to designer or collectible pieces. We quote whichever is higher.
Do you buy Tiffany and Cartier?
Yes — signed designer jewelry often carries premium over melt value.
Do you buy colored gemstones?
Yes — rubies, sapphires, emeralds, and other precious stones when set in quality jewelry.
Do you buy costume jewelry?
We focus on precious metals and genuine gemstones. Costume jewelry without gold/silver content is typically a pass.
How does gold price affect my offer?
We price based on live spot prices at the time of your visit. Gold fluctuates daily.
Do you buy sterling silver flatware?
Yes — forks, knives, spoons, serving pieces, and tea sets. We test purity and weigh everything.
Can I sell broken or single earrings?
Yes. Broken pieces and single earrings still have melt value. We weigh and price everything.

Are you licensed?
