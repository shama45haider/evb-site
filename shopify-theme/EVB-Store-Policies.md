## Refund Policy

**All Sales Are Final**
Because our inventory is made up of pre-owned, vintage, and one-of-a-kind items, we do not accept returns or exchanges. Please review photos, descriptions, and sizing carefully before purchasing. If you have questions about an item, contact us at help@eastvillagebuyers.com before buying.

**Damaged or Incorrect Items**
The only exception is if your item arrives damaged, defective, or is not what you ordered. Contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos so we can make it right — replacement or full refund.

**Authenticity Guarantee**
Every item is authenticated in person by our team before listing. If you believe an item is not authentic as described, contact us immediately for a full investigation and resolution.

## Shipping Policy

**Processing Time**
All orders are processed and shipped within **2 business days** of purchase (Monday–Friday, excluding holidays).

**Shipping Methods & Rates**
We ship via USPS and UPS. Shipping cost is calculated at checkout based on package weight and destination.

**Delivery Times**
Once shipped, most domestic orders arrive within 3–7 business days depending on your location and selected shipping method. You'll receive a tracking number by email as soon as your order ships.

**Local Pickup**
Local customers are welcome to select in-store pickup at checkout and collect their order at 39 Avenue A, New York, NY 10009 during store hours.

**International Shipping**
We currently ship only within the United States.

**Lost or Delayed Packages**
If your tracking shows no movement for more than 5 business days, or your package is marked delivered but not received, contact us at help@eastvillagebuyers.com and we'll help track it down.
